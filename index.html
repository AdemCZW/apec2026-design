02 / 05

About

A two-day, fully in-person seminar in Taipei advancing AI-driven design solutions for MSMEs across APEC economies.

21
APEC Economies
02
Days · Taipei
AI
Design Focus

The APEC seminar is planned as a two-day, fully in-person event held in Taipei in September 2026, aiming to promote AI-driven design solutions that enhance MSMEs' product development efficiency and competitiveness.

It addresses emerging trends, current developments, and practical applications of AI technology adoption in design fields across APEC economies — facilitating knowledge sharing and the exchange of best practices on automated generation and design-process optimization.

Through these exchanges, the event supports MSMEs in improving efficiency, reducing costs, and advancing their digital transformation capabilities.
